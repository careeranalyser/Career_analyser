Personality Test

There are 32 pairs of personality descriptions connected by a five point scale. For each pair, you must choose where on the scale between them you think you are. For example, if the pair is “angry” versus “calm”, you should circle a 1 if you are always angry and never calm, a 3 if you are half and half, etc. On the page after that there are scoring instructions.
